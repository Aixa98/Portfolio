BabyX

By Aixa Benitez on October 26, 2021 • 5 mins read
Scroll

Humans and AI work together

More than seven years ago, Soul Machines pioneered research into the progress of human-machine collaboration by taking a radically different approach. By combining models of physiology, cognition and emotion with advanced and realistic CGI, they created a new form of biologically inspired AI.

BabyX was designed for research and 'she' allows Soul Machines to not only explore models of human behaviour but also create autonomous digital beings. BabyX provides a foundation from which we learn, experiment and continue to develop the world's first end-to-end solution for creating, teaching, managing and deploying digital personas dynamically.

BabyX is based on and builds upon important research in key fields that have been integrated into a cohesive research and development effort. These include: Advanced CGI, Biologically Inspired Cognitive Architectures, Biologically Inspired, Neuroscience, Cognitive Science, Developmental Psychology, Cognitive Linguistics, Affective Computing.

BabyX has its own digital brain that is based on the human brain, allowing it to sense, learn, adapt and communicate interactively in a way that feels alive and engaging.

A key part of the Soul Machines technology is the ability for what they call "Experiential Learning". BabyX learns proactively through direct experience with people and content. By playing interactively with the world around her, she discovers things about it the way we do.
